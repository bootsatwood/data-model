## üìà SCENARIO VARIATIONS

### **Current Revenue Changes Across Scenarios:**
- **S1 ‚Üí S3:** Increases from $160.4M to $177.1M (+$16.7M)
- **Why:** Package enhancements make existing customers more valuable
- **Driven by:** Adding CCM to PCP and MH packages

### **Integration Revenue Changes Across Scenarios:**
- **S1 ‚Üí S3:** Decreases from $164.6M to $148.0M (-$16.6M)
- **Why:** Less room to upsell when packages already enhanced
- **Trade-off:** Current revenue gain = Integration revenue loss

### **New Business Revenue Stays Constant:**
- **All Scenarios:** $4,416.2M (unchanged)
- **Why:** New customers always get full integrated package
- **Implication:** Scenarios only affect current customer value
